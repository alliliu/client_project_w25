Academic Support at UMSI

Welcome! This site contains information about academic advising, academic planning, and student support resources for students in the Master of Science in Information (MSI) Program.

Academic Advising Overview

Academic Advising is a process where students envision, execute, and reflect on their academic plans with guidance from a qualified UMSI Academic Advisor. Students share their goals, challenges, and questions with their academic advisor, and their academic advisor contributes knowledge of university and program requirements, policies, and processes. Together, we develop an academic plan that aligns with the student's personal and professional goals. For information about how to schedule an appointment with your advisor, visit the Connecting with an Advisor page. It may be helpful to review the How to Get the Most Out of Academic Advising page before your appointment.

Understanding the Degree

To earn a Master of Science in Information at the School of Information, you must complete a minimum of 48 credit hours of graduate coursework, with a minimum grade average of B (3.0 minimum cumulative GPA required), including required coursework. Note that within the 48 credit hours of graduate coursework, a minimum of 39 credits must be coursework taken within UMSI (SI credit).

Backpacking and Registration

Step 1: Planning Your Schedule

  1. Visit Atlas to review historical course information (average grade, professor, times, and more).
  2. Access your Degree Audit to review course progress and identify remaining course requirements.
  3. Visit the UMSI Course Catalog to review semester offerings, course descriptions, and most recent syllabi.
  4. Build a sample schedule for the term using Schedule Builder.
  5. Connect with an Academic Advisor to review your plan for the upcoming term.

Step 2: Backpacking

  1. Sign into Wolverine Access
  2. Click on "Student Business" under the "Students" tab.
  3. Click on "Student Center."
  4. Add the courses you have selected for the term to the Backpack.

Step 3: Registration

  1. Sign into Wolverine Access
  2. Click on "Student Business" under the "Students" tab.
  3. Click on "Student Center."
  4. Select the "check box" next to the courses that you have added to your Backpack
  5. Click "Proceed to Step 2 of 3"
  6. Click "Finish Registration"
  7. Confirm that there is a green checkmark next to the course for which you intend to register. If there is a red "X," review the notification and proceed appropriately.
  8. View your schedule by selecting "My Course Schedule" within the Student Center.
